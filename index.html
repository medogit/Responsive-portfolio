About Me

Born and raised in Morocco, I came to America in search of the "American Dream". I soon stumbled upon Dunkin' Donuts.. Mmmm. I realized in order to be able to afford my Dunkin' cravings I would need to find a suitable job. After graduating UNH with a Bio-medical Science Degree, I found out that my love for donuts was at odds with health work. For this reason I decided to look elsewhere for happiness.

I came upon a UNH sponsored program to learn how to code for the web. And here I am today fullfilling my dream to be a developer for Dunkin' with free unlimited donuts in their office.
